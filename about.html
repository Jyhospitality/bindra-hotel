The hotel's accommodations include a restaurant, free private parking, a bar, and a shared lounge. The accommodation features room service, 24- hours front desk, and currency exchange. In the hotel, a flat-screen TV and a wardrobe are provided in every room. There will be refrigerators in each unit for visitors. Breakfast is available as a buffet or continental at Hotel Bindra's Supremacy. While the business center has newspapers, a fax machine, and a photocopier, there is also a tour desk and car rentals are offered there.
